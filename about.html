About The Organization




WHO ARE WE?

Vesh Foundation, is a non- profit, voluntary organization in Kenya established in 2016. Vesh Foundation of Kenya has been working to spread awareness, prompt early detection, and ensure proper treatment and rehabilitation. It is also involved providing interventions to people struggling with Alchohol and Substance abuse. It has fast growing volunteer network composed of people from different age group and socio-economic which is vital for sustained development and success. The foundation exists to provide Comprehensive health programs that are integrated and coordinated approach to reducing cancer incidence, Alcohol and substance abuse.VESH FOUNDATION was created in 2017 and registered as a foundation No. R50723 to be involved in all aspects of Diseases prevention, control, and care

Vision

People working together to transform environments and conditions to assure health and well-being for all.

Mission

The mission of the Vesh Foundation is to supporting community health and development through collaborative research and evaluation, teaching and training, and technical support and capacity building.
